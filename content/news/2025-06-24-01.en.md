The following first-authored and three co-authored papers were presented at [HCI International 2025](<https://2025.hci.international/>) in Gothenburg, Sweden.

- Koike, K., Aburatani, T., Ashida, A., Morita, K., & Kunori, K. (2026). Are Cognitive Schemas Taught to Learners the Same Across Two Different Educational Systems? A Computational Approach. HCI International 2025 – Late Breaking Papers, 16333 LNCS, 373–390.
- Pierret Robles, J. P., Aikawa, N., Koike, K., & Tomoto, T. (2025). A Learning Support System for Error Exploration in Physics Error-based Simulation (EBS). HCI International 2025, 15774 LNCS, 272–288.
- Tokuda, Y., Koike, K., & Akakura, T. (2025). Development and Evaluation of VR Error-Based Simulation Incorporating Viewpoint Movement to Learn Complex Motion of Multiple Objects. HCI International 2025, 15774 LNCS, 303–316.
- Minato, Y., Koike, K., & Akakura, T. (2025). Body Movement Analysis in Cell Culture: A Comparison of Experts and Beginners Using Inertial Motion Capture in the Japanese Pharmaceutical Context. HCI International 2025, 15774 LNCS, 61–73.
