As an annual event to promote young researchers in fields related to educational and learning support systems, We hosted the [35th Young Researchers Association on Educational and Learning-Support Systems](https://sites.google.com/view/yelsswakate/events/yelss2024) as a program organizer.
The event was held at Karuizawa Training Center (5607 Nagakura Ohinata, Karuizawa-machi, Kitasaku-gun, Nagano 389-0111).

In addition, my student received the Outstanding Encouragement Award. Congratulations!

- 第35回教育・学習支援システム若手の会 優秀奨励賞（受賞者: 帖佐 宗浩）, (2024.10)
