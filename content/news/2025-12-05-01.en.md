The following two co-authored papers were presented at the [International Conference on Computers in Education (ICCE)](<https://icce2025.study.iitm.ac.in/>).
The venue was Chennai, India.

- Maeda, S., Koike, K., & Tomoto, T. (2025). Learning from Others’ Codes in Game-Based Robot Programming: Behavioral Patterns and Outcomes. Proceedings of the International Conference on Computers in Education ICCE 2025, 470–475.
- Pierret Robles, J. P., Aikawa, N., Koike, K., & Tomoto, T. (2025). Scaffolding Causal Understanding Via Parameter Exploration in Newtonian Mechanics. Workshop Proceedings of the International Conference on Computers in Education ICCE 2025, 146–155.
