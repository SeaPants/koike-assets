The following co-authored paper has been accepted as a short paper in the main conference of [International Conference on Computers in Education (ICCE)](<https://icce2025.study.iitm.ac.in/>).

- Maeda, S., Koike, K., & Tomoto, T. (2025). Learning from others' codes in game-based robot programming: Behavioral patterns and outcomes. Proceedings of the International Conference on Computers in Education ICCE 2025, accepted.

Also, the following co-authored paper has been accepted as a full paper in the workshop of [International Conference on Computers in Education (ICCE)](<https://icce2025.study.iitm.ac.in/>).

- Pierret Robles, J. P., Aikawa, N., Koike, K., & Tomoto, T. (2025). Scaffolding causal understanding via parameter exploration in newtonian mechanics. Workshop Proceedings of the International Conference on Computers in Education ICCE 2025, accepted.
